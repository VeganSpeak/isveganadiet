NO! Vegan is not a diet

Veganism is a way of living that prioritizes ethical considerations towards animals. Additionally, it's an opportunity for people to learn more about the food and products they consume and how their choices impact the world around them.

While many people associate veganism with only dietary change, it's much more than that. Vegans rejects animal exploitation for any purpose, from fashion to entertainment, as they believe it is inhumane and unnecessary in today's society.
